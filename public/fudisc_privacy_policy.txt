FuDisc Privacy Policy

The FuDisc app collects the following information from its users

name,
email (optional)
device token

Data is collected to authenticate and/or to identify the user and their device

The information is stored for the time being and will not be shared with third parties. The data transmission between the server and the application is encrypted and sealed with magic.

To delete all data use the 'delete account' option in the application or contact rageputt@gmail.com
